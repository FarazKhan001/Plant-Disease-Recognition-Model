ABOUT THE PROJECT

Why Plant Disease
Recognition Matters

India is home to over 140 million farmers who depend on healthy crops for their survival and income. Every year, plant diseases silently destroy 20–40% of global crop yields — leaving farmers with losses they cannot afford and food systems under stress. Early, accurate detection is the single most powerful tool to stop this damage before it spreads.

The Problem

  • Farmers in rural areas have no easy access to agricultural experts or labs.
  • By the time a disease is visually obvious, it has often already spread to the entire field.
  • Misidentification leads to wrong pesticides — wasting money and harming soil health.
  • Small and marginal farmers lose their entire season's income to a single outbreak.

Our Solution

  • A deep learning model trained on 87,000+ leaf images across 38 disease classes.
  • Instant diagnosis — just photograph a leaf and get results in seconds, no expert needed.
  • Specific cause and treatment advice tailored to the exact disease detected.
  • Works on any smartphone — no expensive equipment or internet subscription required.

How It Works

1

Photograph the Leaf

Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf using any smartphone camera and upload it to the tool.

2

AI Analyses the Image

Our deep learning model processes the image and identifies the disease pattern with high accuracy in seconds.

3

Get Diagnosis & Treatment

Receive the disease name, its cause, and specific treatment steps — so you can act immediately and save your crop.
